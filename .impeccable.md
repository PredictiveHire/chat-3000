## Design Context

### Users
Job candidates going through an AI-led chat interview on behalf of companies using Sapia.ai. Used on both desktop and mobile, often in a focused, slightly high-stakes context — someone applying for a real role. The experience should feel trustworthy, calm, and human — not robotic or stressful.

### Brand Personality
Warm, considered, approachable. Three words: **calm, human, clear**. The UI should feel like a thoughtful recruiter, not a bureaucratic form. Emotional goal: put the candidate at ease so they can express themselves honestly.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Soft lavender-pink accent (`#ffceff`, oklch-tinted neutrals toward hue 320). Manrope typeface — geometric but warm. Rounded, friendly bubble shapes. Subtle shadows rather than hard borders. Clean whitespace. No dark-mode glows, no aggressive color contrasts.

### Design Principles
1. **Human over robotic** — Motion and timing should feel conversational, like a real person typing, not a machine executing.
2. **Calm confidence** — Nothing should feel rushed or jittery. Animations are gentle, purposeful, and quick to get out of the way.
3. **Progressive reveal** — Only show what's needed now. Inputs appear only after the interviewer has finished speaking.
4. **Accessible by default** — All motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`. No animation is load-bearing for understanding.
5. **Mobile-first** — The full-screen mobile experience is the primary surface; desktop is an enhancement.
